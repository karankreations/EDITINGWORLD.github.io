STORY BEHIND "KARANKREATION"
My name is Parminder Kumar. Jalandhar, the most popular city of Punjab is my hometown. I am 20 years old. Graphic designing is my passion, to become a successful graphic designer in the future is the aim of my life But setting up the goal is not the only job. I have to work hard for this.
It all started in 2017 when I was studying in my matrics class I heard for the first time about vector art. I do the photography every weekend with my canon 1500d camera know about adobe photoshop & how to use it because I did edit my portraits in that software. Afterward, I bought the adobe illustrator membership for just a month also took online classes to learn this software. At the end of the day, I did manage to learn all the essential tools. Initially, I chose raw images from the royalty-free website for working on them, the results were outstanding then I worked out on my portraits & made the very first vector art of my face and that art got me everything till date. Then i gave myself a brand name "KARANKREATIONS" as my nickname is Karan because of this I got thousands of compliments at the age of 17.
After a while, I got excellence in illustrator also and worked as a digital marketer for a few companies. Then I finally got my aim of becoming a successful graphic designer.
